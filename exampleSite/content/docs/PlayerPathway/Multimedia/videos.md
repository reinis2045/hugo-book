## Edit

1. Videos >
2. Click on folder.
3. Open video by clicking on name.
4. Rewrite Name and Description.
5. Update video.

> **Tip**
>
> Videos are hosted, uploaded and managed on Vimeo.
> Information about videos are periodically imported from Vimeo into the Platform.
